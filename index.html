Hi, I'm Bamba.

Student at Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute (RPI) and Fabricator from TinyWPA.

After beginning as an intern through my high school in 2022, I got hired as a fabrication intern by TinyWPA to continue developing my skills. I started off with smaller responsibilities like rough cut milling, sanding, and less detailed finishing.


Eventually, I progressed to aiding in leading major projects in the Summer of 2025, such as playful learning structures at Logan Library in Philadelphia.

Summer '25 | Leadership & Fabrication

Logan Library

Co-led design and construction efforts for community projects. Fabricated 15 structural frames and prepared 250+ wood components for assembly. Co-directed community build sessions, guiding interns and summer camp participants through hands-on construction tasks.

Prototype Phase: Strength and weight testing frames.
The beginnings: Clamping and assembling frames with the team.
Finished Project: "Pollination in Logan" with seating and interactive elements.

Welsh Fountain

Initial scale modeling and prototyping.

Summer '25 | Restoration

Rail Park Renovation

Due to unforeseen and unfortunate circumstances, one of the seating arrangements at the Rail Park in Center City, Philadelphia, caught fire, and the ending portion was unusable. TinyWPA was tasked with replacing the charred slats. I was charged with remaking the attachment slits on each piece and aided in the renovation.

Replacing the burned framing underneath the slats.
Installing new slats and adding backrest skeletons.
The finished product after renovation.
Summer '23 & '25 | Civic Design

Swim Philly

Swim Philly is a project that TinyWPA started in 2023 in collaboration with the city of Philadelphia to make pools in underserved neighborhoods look more welcoming and inviting. We contributed coroplast figures on fences and installed seating areas to bring families together.

Building the internal structure of the island seating.
Delivery of components to the job site.
Final look of the heart-shaped deck at J. Finnegan Pool.
